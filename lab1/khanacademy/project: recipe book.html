Chicken Plov

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 3–4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 2 cups
Chicken 300 g
Carrot 1 big
Oil 2 tbsp

Step 1: Cut chicken and vegetables.

Step 2: Fry chicken in oil.

Step 3: Add rice and water.

Step 4: Cook for 20 minutes and serve.
