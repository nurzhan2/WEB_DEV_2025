New Zealand is a country in the southwest Pacific Ocean, near Australia. It has two main islands: the North Island and the South Island. The capital city is Wellington, and the largest city is Auckland. About 5 million people live there. The main languages are English and Māori. The currency is the New Zealand dollar (NZD). New Zealand is famous for its beautiful nature — mountains, lakes, beaches, and forests. Many scenes from The Lord of the Rings were filmed there. The country is also known for rugby, sheep farming, and clean air. It is a peaceful and safe place to live and travel.
